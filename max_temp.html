Maximum Temp Vs Latitude

As you can see her the max temperature for the recordings are pretty high in the negative latitudes and stays pretty consistant as we approach the equator. Then the further north we get the maximum temperature decreases. This is most likely due to the earths tiled axis that changes for summer and winter. Summer happens when the earths axis causes a hemisphere to be closer to the sun. Resulting in higher temps and if one hemisphere is closer to the sun then the other is further away resulting in lower temps.

All that said I believe this graph is showing that its currently summer in the southern hemisphere. and since the equater is towrds the center of our latitudes (i.e latitudes close to zero) they are pretty well regulated in temp and stay about the same all year
