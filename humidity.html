Humidity Vs Latitude

This graph shows us the relative humidity in all our different latitudes. We removed all cities that have humidity more than 100%. As you can see a majority of our latitudes have random humidity readings. It appears that there is no relation to latitudes and relative humidity

Although the humidities seem to be random there does appear to be a strange pattern with the some of the humidity recordings between 80 and 90 degress latitude where there is a pattern of 0% humidity readings. I believe that some more research is required on those since these could be false or null values

The graph above has the latitudes on the X Axis with negative latitudes representing southern hemisphere and positive latitudes representing northern hemispher. The values on the Y Axis represent the humidity recorded on that date.
