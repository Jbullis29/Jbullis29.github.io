Windspeed Vs Latitude

This graph shows us the sustained winds on the day that we requested the data. From this graph you can see that wind really isnt determined by latitude.

The graph above has the latitudes on the X Axis with negative latitudes representing southern hemisphere and positive latitudes representing northern hemispher. The values on the Y Axis represent sustained windspeed recorded on that date.
